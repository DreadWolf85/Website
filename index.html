FINAL FANTASY 7


Final Fantasy7 started out as one game before turning into a collection of games a movie and an anime series, this collection of media has served to expand the lore of Final Fantasy 7 as a whole. The compilation encompasses several sequels, prequels, and spin-offs in one sprawling timeline, and each story serves to give different characters their own spotlight and further enrich the universe of Final Fantasy 7. As this universe continues to expand into uncharted territory with the upcoming Final Fantasy 7 Rebirth, it's time to take a quick look at three Final Fantasy 7 games, the original Final Fantasy 7, Final Fantasy 7 Remake and the upcoming Final Fantasy 7 Rebirth.
